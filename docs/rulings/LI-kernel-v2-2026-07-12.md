## Rulings

1. **D1 — the family basis is the locked FCBI v0.5 distance basis,
   reused.**  For each schedule and target, the kernel is the nonnegative
   target-specific distance `d_i = min over enumerated float paths
   containing i of (path margin − driving margin)`, `d ≥ 0`,
   `w = 2^(−d/λ) ∈ (0, 1]`, margins in fixed reference hours over discrete
   members only, with the PROVEN λ-invariant convergence frontier
   (`FCBI_CONV_LAMBDA`/`FCBI_CONV_TOL`), the exact `FCBI_PATHS_MAX` cap
   (a hit → PROVISIONAL, disclosed), propagated non-target governance
   (`_governed_codes`, quarantined + disclosed), and the stable
   completion-milestone target (`_resolve_fcbi_target`).  Implemented as
   `_KernelV2` / `_v2_enumerate` / `_build_kernel_v2`
   (`analytics/li_indices.py`), a mirror of the locked `_target_distance`
   round structure that additionally collects per-path margins for PCI and
   accepts a UID target (BWI's pinned anchor).  Per-metric targets: the
   family completion milestone for PCI/CDI/RDI; the UID-pinned bow-wave
   anchor for BWI (its own `_build_kernel_v2` map).
2. **D2 — severity beside, never inside.**  Per update,
   `N = max(0, −target margin)` (reference working days of deficit) and
   `ΔN⁺` deepening are reported BESIDE PCI and CDI (`_v2_severity`,
   `PciResult.n_severity/n_deepening`, `CdiResult.n_severity`) — the
   signal the abolished w > 1 premium was groping toward, carried
   explicitly (FCBI O2/Q5 precedent).
3. **D3 — CDI dwell accrues while live.**  A completed activity retains
   every dwell unit earned while live (retrospective criticality-time =
   accumulated live history, ruling C2's rationale) but stops EARNING
   after completion — a recorded supersession of the v0.4 incidental
   post-completion accrual through the own-float fallback.  Milestone
   markers are likewise superseded out of the dwell allocation (references,
   not work; their pressure reaches the analysis via governance quarantine
   and the severity strip).
4. **D4 — LI-04 provisional/ungraded.**  The two-sided scored anchors
   [0.05, 0.15, 0.35, 0.6] were tuned to a floor-0.1, top-10,
   premium-priced scale that no longer exists; `scorecard.yaml` LI-04 now
   carries a `provisional: true` block and the metric is ungraded pending
   recalibration against real series (LI-01 precedent).  LI-05/LI-09 stay
   graded (units/scale survive; pinned demo letters verified).  LI-07
   remains unscored.

## What the new basis abolishes (audit K1/K2/K5 + residuals)

- **K1 — own-total-float fallback.**  An activity on no enumerated path is
  UNRESOLVED: quarantined and counted/disclosed per metric, never priced at
  its own TF (before: an orphan task with no logic entered the board at
  rf 4.0 / weight 0.574).
- **K2 — the w > 1 negative-float premium.**  Distances are nonnegative, so
  w ≤ 1 always.  Honest record: a DRIVER-ONLY deepening still moves PCI
  (0.556 → 0.802 on the audit probe) — legitimately, because the other
  paths' distances to the deepened driver grew (relative separation, not a
  premium).  The pinned anchor is UNIFORM deepening (every margin shifts
  −10/−5/−10 vs 0/5/0): PCI is IDENTICAL and the severity strip carries
  N = 10.0 (`test_v2_pci_no_negative_float_premium`).
- **K5 — the raw top-10 truncation.**  `KERNEL_PATHS_N = 10` is out of the
  pipeline; the proven frontier + exact cap replace it.  PCI's 1/10
  Herfindahl floor is gone (the dead scored-anchor range below 0.1 no
  longer exists — hence D4).
- **PCI weight residual (deferred in v0.4.2/1c, closed here).**  Herfindahl
  weights read the discrete-only fixed-reference margins, so a kept mixed
  path's LOE member no longer leaks into the weights.
- **λ governance.**  λ is bounded to (0, `FCBI_CONV_LAMBDA`] for the same
  frontier-validity reason as FCBI (W4-05); an invalid λ leaves PCI/CDI NOT
  EVALUATED with the reason (RDI/BWI do not weight by λ).  The Q-H
  sensitivity sets re-point to the new basis unchanged.

## The honest-degradation rule (found while landing)

On the demo series the AUTO-RESOLVED family target is MS-100 while A1200's
MANDFIN deadline governs the entire network: every live activity is
quarantined as governed.  The v0.4 kernel would have priced them all on
own-float; a naive v2 would have read required = 0 (fabricated "no debt")
and an empty CDI.  Landed rule: `eligible == 0 and quarantined > 0` →
required None for that update; a series of Nones → NOT EVALUATED with the
quarantine named in the reason (rubric A1).  Regression:
`test_family_auto_target_full_quarantine_is_honest`; the `indices_a1200`
fixture mirrors the FCBI demo test's analyst-selected target for the
computable cases.
