# LI-06 BDI — Wave-4 governed revision (ruling Q-G: fixed basis + LOE out)

**Status:** accepted · **Date:** 2026-07-12 · **Principal:** Alex Bachowski
(Wave-4 triage on the LI-02..LI-10 audit, question Q-G — "Fixed basis + LOE
out") · **Classification: number-changing** (LI-06 is scored, weight 2; the
share moves on any series with in-progress or LOE steps on the driving
path).

## Rulings

1. **Fixed length basis (audit BDI-2, rubric A4).**  Every driving-path step
   weighs its ORIGINAL (planned) duration.  The prior remaining-else-original
   basis shrank an in-progress original step as it burned down while added
   steps held full length, so the "dilution" share rose with mere progress —
   execution and revision conflated (probe: original 20-day step at 4 days
   remaining vs a 10-day added step read 71.4% "post-baseline"; the fixed
   basis reads 33.3% at every progress state).
2. **LOE steps zero-length (audit BDI-3 — the deferred family ruling).**
   LOE/WBS-summary/hammock steps on the tool-of-record driving path
   contribute zero length to both numerator and denominator (not discrete
   work); they remain listed as steps.  An all-LOE/milestone path therefore
   has no length basis and reads NOT EVALUATED per the sentinel ruling.
3. **Explicit baseline / target parameters + disclosure (audit BDI-4,
   rubric B4).**  `baseline_dilution_index(sa, baseline_index=0,
   target_code=None)`: the baseline defaults to the series' first schedule
   and the target auto-resolves on the latest schedule, but both are
   parameters, both choices are NAMED in the standing disclosures (with a
   confirm-for-work-product note when defaulted), and an out-of-range
   baseline_index degrades with a reason, never raises.
4. **Hardening/disclosure (audit BDI-5).**  The first-appearance
   attribution's coupling to the change register's detail-string format is
   regression-locked (`test_bdi5_logic_change_detail_format_coupling_lock`);
   the "NOT FOUND — REVIEW" non-fabricating default is retained; standing
   disclosures state the length basis, baseline/target provenance, and
   code-keyed matching.
